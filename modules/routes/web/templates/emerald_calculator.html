About Wynncraft Currency

Wynncraft uses a base-64 currency system:

  • 1 stx (Stack of Liquid Emeralds) = 64 le = 4,096 eb = 262,144 e
  • 1 le (Liquid Emerald) = 64 eb = 4,096 e
  • 1 eb (Emerald Block) = 64 e
  • 1 e (Emerald) = base unit
This calculator helps convert between the raw emerald count and the formatted Wynncraft notation.
